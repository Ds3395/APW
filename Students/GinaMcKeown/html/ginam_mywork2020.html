CryptoTradingBot (Second Semester Project)
Summary:
A python program that accesses the prices of a cryptocurrency and uses the RSI (Relative Strength Indicator) to recommend times to buy and sell. The program sends text reminders, but can also automatically buy and sell cryptocurrency of a certain amount in USD. It checks all the closing prices of each candlestick, and uses them to measure the RSI, once the RSI is below 30 or above 70, the program will act according ly(buying or selling). The program acts through binance, but sends SMS messages through twilio. Orginal Goals:
  • Automatic trading with crypto
  • Real live data
  • User alerts


Future Goals:
  • Connect to web app for user interface
  • Offer email notifications


Outcome:
I am happy with how this turned out. The texting aspect took longer than I expected, and though it's been resolved, I am not sure what the problem exactly was. I met the majority of my goals when heading into this project, but after creating the CryptoWebApp (because I wanted a visual side), I have new goals for a combined version.

What I learned:
I learned about how candlestick charts work, with high, low, open, and closing prices. At first, I thought it was a bit confusing, but it makes a lot of sense for values that are constantly changing. I also learned about how RSI works, and I hope to implement more theories into my program to make it more reliabley profitable. In addition, I learned more about collecting data and specifcally using the binance APIs.
