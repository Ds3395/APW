Last Year's Project

For my project, I want to make a multiplayer 2D or 2.5D (3D models in a 2D space) fighting game like Guilty Gear -Strive- or BlazBlue Centralfiction with Visual Novel elements for storytelling in C# using Unity. This has been a passion project of mine for a while, and I want my game to create cool moments, and generate a sizable dedicated community like with Street Fighter Third Strike and Evo Moment 37. I want to add a cast of unique characters that are all fun to play, and embody the different fighting styles throughout the genre's history. Along with this cast, I want to add unique system/universal mechanics for this game as well as adding a Roman Cancel system (from Guilty Gear) as I really enjoy using it. I also want to add a soundtrack reminiscent of my favorite entries to the genre, as well as maps that take place at beautiful, stylistic vistas. If I have time, along with the fighting game itself, I want to add a story mode that really works to add more depth to the characters.

DeathBlow

A 2D fighting game that is based on a more volatile and deliberate kind of combat. Taking heavy influence from games like Sekiro and Guilty Gear Strive, DeathBlow will focus on blocking and parrying blows until the opportunity arrives to strike. Landing a solid blow will deal a significant amount of damage, and may grant you a deathblow at a low enough health percentage. By parrying or chipping away at their block, you can get your opponent in a stun and land a final blow. The game focuses on being aggressive but punishes overaggression. This game will implement rollback networking code that allows the game to feel responsive and fun. This game is more of a side project to Arachnid, but it holds a very special place in my heart.

Arachnid

Arachnid is a crossplay VR and PC multiplayer survival horror game that focuses on creating a sense of dread for the player. This project has been divided among three people in a way that allows them to each have a separate project that combines into one larger product at the end. In this game, you will be a group of exterminators sent to eliminate the Arachnid, a mysterious creature that lurks in the dark... While the lore and exact gameplay have not been fully planned out yet, the core idea is that the player will be navigating environments with several other players in order to hunt this creature. There will be several other creatures that the character has to deal with, and they have to manage their limited resources in order to survive. Check out the game at: Arachnid.Itch.io. It will most likely be on Steam at some point, and you can check out the devlog channel at: Swanky Studios.
